BOARD REPORT - October 2008

Community
  • The ActiveMQ project has had another very busy quarter. Jonathan Anstey was voted in as an ActiveMQ committer.
  • The development and user lists continue to stay vibrant.
Development
  • Lots of development effort continues around pushing out new versions of ActiveMQ, ActiveMQ-CPP, and Camel. A new 5.2.0 release of ActiveMQ is currently in the works.
Releases
  • ActiveMQ-CPP 2.2.1
  • Camel 1.4.0
