ActiveMQ Classic 6.0.0 Release

Home › News › ActiveMQ Classic 6.0.0 Release
Nov 17nd, 2023

Apache ActiveMQ Classic was released on .

It’s a major release for the project, especially bringing:

  • Partial Jakarta Messaging 3.1 & JMS 2.0 support (new features will come in the 6.x series)
  • Jakarta EE namespace support
  • JDK17/20/21 support
  • Spring 6.x support
  • Jetty 11.x support
  • Apache Camel 4.x support
  • Jolokia 2.x support
  • and much more !

You can find details on the release notes.
